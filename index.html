ECHO 5

The main case of our SFF line, designed for maximum airflow at low noise levels. The ECHO 5 is the result of a year of research and simulations. The current version (v0.10) has about 5.6L being one of the smallest cases on the market to support a fullsize GPU (278mm).

First Prototype

September 2019

The Alpha concept


Designed for simple systems, without video cards. With only 4 Liters, the project used low consumption parts and was made with a 3D printer. The project had several failures mainly due to its high temperatures.

Alpha 18 Liter Prototype

March 2020

The Bravo concept


The idea of this model was to use conventional parts and still reduce the volume. With 18 Liters, the project solved the temperature problem of the previous one, but greatly increased the total volume.

Bravo 14 Liter Prototype

April 2020

The Charlie concept


With 4 Liters less, the Charlie Concept was just a rearrangement of the pieces inside the chassis. There were no changes in compatibility, but the simulations show problems in airflow.

Team formation

June 2020

Team formation


The formation of the team was an essential factor in the development of the project. Now with the focus on the creation of the site and dissemination to raise funds for improvement of the project.

Delta 12, Echo 7


By changing the compatibility of parts we were able to achieve an even smaller volume and solve the airflow of the previous model, resulting in the 12 Liters of Delta Concept. In the same period we worked on the idea of a much smaller case with 7 Liters and an even more restricted compatibility of parts.

ECHO 5

July 2020

That would be the name of the 5th concept that was chosen as the focus of the SFF project. After going through a rigorous series of redesigns and simulations, it proved to be extremely promising. With the materials purchased, this was the second concept to become a physical prototype with its first version being made by a 3D printer. In the same month, the project went through another series of 6 revisions that improved several factors, but mainly the cooling. Translated with www.DeepL.com/Translator (free version)

PRE-PRODUCTION AND MODULAR CONCEPT

August 2020

After 3 more revisions, the Prototype Echo 5 V0.09 was sent to production, 3 models were requested in different metals and paintings. During the same period the concepts of modules emerged, thus increasing the range of the product to be more flexible in relation to its capabilities.
With the prototype in production we started to plan and produce the site to divulge the project in a more detailed way, besides importing all the specialized material that would make possible the production of the 5 liter volume. RTX Hype

September 2020

With the announcement of a new series of video cards by the manufacturer Nvidia, the project was frozen. The 3000 generation presented by Nvidia was completely incompatible with Echo 5, only after 14 days and a series of changes Echo 5 v0.1 began to take shape. With the increased compatibility, the new version is a direct improvement in almost all points compared to the previous version.
