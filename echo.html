SFF LINE UP (under development)


Cooling Module

Echo Cooling Module is a 92x184mm AIO watercooler that can be connected to ECHO 5 to add 200w of cooling potential per module, serving for the processor or video card. Each module adds about 1.2L to the case.

Cooling Module +

With some adjustments, the Echo Cooling Plus Module is an improved version of its brother, being also a WaterCooler AIO of 92x184mm with a more powerful pump and able to accommodate up to 4 Fans, its cooling potential is 280w per module. Each module adds about 1.5L to the case.

Creator Module

The creator module adds faster network, more SSD mounts, type C USB, SD and micro SD card reader, usb 3.0. Is the perfect module for photographers and filmmakers that need these extra connectors.

NAS/Backup Module (concept)

Converting the M.2 connector to PCI 4x to add a RAID Card with space to mount three 3.5” HDDs and up to six 2.5” SSDs. But for now it is only a concept.

Other products:

At the moment the whole focus is on the launch of Echo 5 and its modules. However, other models are under monitoring such as a model of mATX and Mini ITX Low Profile. Products such as keyboard, mouse, monitor, are also being considered.
