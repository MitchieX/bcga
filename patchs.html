Update v.0.10

1. Motherboard tray is now modular.

New internal positions for the tray allowing greater compatibility of CPU air coolers and GPUs from slot 2.2 to 2.7.

2. Dimensions reverted from 275x218x100mm to 285x200x100mm.

Reversion from the previous version. Still subject to change.

3. Intake fans removed for compatibility with Echo modules.

Required for operation of the lower modules with quick connection.

4. Internal and external positioning of the changed power cable.

Changing the cable route and external position of the C14 connector improving compatibility with tray modularity.

5. Volume reduction from 6.5L to 5.6L

Direct consequence of the reversal of dimensions. Returning to the 5 Liters house.

6. 0.5mm standoffs for the motherboard removed.

Use of 2mm nylon spacers added to the assembly.

7. General review. Changes based on suggestions from the community.

Revised product, many changes planned for internal modularity.

Update v.0.09

1. Dimensions increased from 275x210x100mm to 275x218x100mm.

The increase in size is temporary and its function is to test the available space and check the assembly steps. It is very important that the model is easy to assemble and disassemble.

2. Power button relocated from the top of the case to the front.

Button was moved to the front of the chassis, allowing the modules to be fitted without the need for changes in the cable routes.

3. Added 5mm standoffs for the motherboard and riser cable PCB.

Spacers were added to provide space between the motherboard and GPU backplates. This space helps to remove a heat bag that was previously trapped in an area of turbulent air.

4. Fan mounting improved.

Minor changes in the fan cutouts, in addition to the countersunk in the plate for a better finished assembly outside the case.

5. Power button altered from 22mm to 12mm in diameter

Reduced button to be able to accommodate in the new location without interfering the GPU power cables and to maintain the minimalist aesthetic.

6. Volume increase from 5.7L to 6.5L

Direct consequence of the increase in dimensions, with the improvement of the project this change must be reversed.

7. Project sent for pre-production.

Project sent for pre-production in different thicknesses of aluminum and steel in order to test the structural strengths and weight of each model.
